mindPick
The Knowledge Monetization Layer for the AI Age
The Problem
"Can I pick your brain?" messages create constant context switching and unpaid labor. Scheduling calls introduces friction, and whatever gets shared is lost in a one-off conversation. Experts need a professional, scalable way to say yes—without calls, back-and-forth, or awkward payment asks.
The Solution
Async, paid video Q&A platform. Askers pay upfront, record or write their question, and experts reply with a short video or voice note within a stated SLA—no meetings required. Payments authorized at submit, captured on delivery. Experts own their content in a private, searchable knowledge base, creating reusable leverage instead of disposable calls.

Knowledge Graph Moat
